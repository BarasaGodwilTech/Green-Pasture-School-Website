Our Story & Mission

Rooted in faith. Built for community transformation.

Since 1998

Green Pasture Christian Outreach

Green Pasture Christian Outreach started in 1998 with a vision to empower the poverty-stricken communities of Maluku, Mbale. Inspired by Psalms 23:1-2, the name “Green Pasture” was received as a prayer answered, and it became a symbol of hope and restoration.

In 2001, Green Pasture pioneered free secondary education in Mbale, breaking barriers for rural children. Today we continue to provide quality education that blends academics, vocational skills, leadership formation and Christian values.

We believe education is not just about certificates. It is about discovering God-given talents, developing character, and building skills for meaningful work and service.

Mission, Vision & Values

What guides our work every day

Mission

To provide quality secondary and vocational education that empowers rural communities through skills, discipline and faith-based values.

Vision

To be a leading center of holistic education where learners become responsible citizens, entrepreneurs and community builders.

Values

Faith, excellence, unity, service, integrity, self-reliance and stewardship.

Approach

Balanced academics, practical skills training, mentorship and leadership development.
