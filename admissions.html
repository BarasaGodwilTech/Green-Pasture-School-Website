What you need

Entry Requirements

S.1 (O' Level)

PLE results slip and a recommendation letter. Students join a supportive learning environment with strong discipline.

S.2 - S.4

Previous school report, transfer letter and interview assessment (where applicable).

S.5 (A' Level)

UCE results slip and subject combination counselling to guide learners into strong pathways.

Vocational

Open to school leavers and community learners. Basic literacy recommended; practical interest required.

How to Apply

Simple steps to join Green Pasture

Step 1: Contact the school through phone or email for guidance and available spaces.

Step 2: Visit the campus with required documents for registration and orientation.

Step 3: Receive joining instructions and term opening dates.

Step 4: Join school and begin your learning journey in academics and skills training.
