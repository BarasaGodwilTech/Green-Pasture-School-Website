20+ Years of Excellence

Empowering Rural Communities Through Education

Founded in 1998, Green Pasture Christian Outreach began with a vision to empower the poverty-stricken communities of Maluku, Mbale. From a humble dream rooted in Psalms 23:1-2, we have grown into a beacon of hope, providing quality secondary and vocational education to thousands.

We were the first in Mbale to offer free secondary education in 2001, breaking barriers and transforming lives. Today, we continue to nurture God-given talents, instilling entrepreneurship, self-discipline, and a positive attitude towards work.
